Frequently asked questions

Do I need prior yoga experience?

No. All our courses are designed to welcome absolute beginners. The Philosophy and Pranayama courses require no physical yoga practice at all.

Can I practice with a serious injury or illness?

Yes — this is exactly who our Adaptive Yoga course is designed for. We ask that you share relevant information so we can advise appropriately. Please also keep your healthcare provider informed.

Are the courses live or recorded?

It varies by course. Philosophy and Pranayama courses are self-paced with audio/video content. Adaptive Yoga includes live online sessions with recorded replays. Details are listed on each course page.

What technology do I need?

A phone, tablet, or computer with a working speaker or headphones. For live sessions, a stable internet connection and a quiet space. No special software required.

How much do courses cost?

Pricing varies. The Pranayama Library is free. Course fees are discussed individually — we aim to keep costs accessible and offer reduced rates where needed. Please ask.
