Courses & Programs

All courses are available online. Each is designed to be accessible regardless of physical ability, offered at your own pace.

Our courses range from introductory explorations of yoga philosophy to dedicated pranayama practice programs and adaptive movement sequences for those with physical limitations.

All sessions include PDF notes, audio or video guidance, and access to our practitioner community. You may enrol individually in any course or contact us to discuss a personalised learning path.

Foundation

Introduction to Yoga Philosophy

6 weeks · Online · Self-paced

An accessible, non-dogmatic introduction to the philosophical foundations of yoga. We explore the Yoga Sutras of Patanjali, the concept of polarity and unity, the eight limbs, and the key philosophical vocabulary of the tradition.

No prior knowledge required. The course is text-based with weekly audio reflections and guided breath sessions.

Includes: 6 weekly modules · PDF readings · Audio reflections · Discussion forum

All levels

Pranayama — The Science of Breath

8 weeks · Online · Audio-guided

A complete pranayama training from the ground up. Beginning with natural breath awareness and progressing through Ujjayi, Nadi Shodhana, Kapalabhati, Bhramari, and complete yogic breathing.

Each week includes a theory session, a guided audio practice, and integration notes. Suitable for practitioners with physical limitations — all sessions can be practiced seated or lying down.

Specialised

Adaptive Yoga for Healing

10 weeks · Small group · Online live

Specifically designed for practitioners managing chronic pain, injury, neurological conditions, fatigue syndromes, post-surgical recovery, or age-related limitations. A deeply holistic course integrating breath, gentle movement, philosophy, and contemplation.

Every practice is fully adaptable — nothing is required that the body cannot offer. Sessions can be practiced entirely from a chair or bed.

Includes: 10 live sessions · Recorded replays · Full adaptations guide · One personal consultation

Intermediate

Yoga Sutras — Deep Study

12 weeks · Reading group · Online

A close reading of Patanjali's Yoga Sutras in four chapters. We work through the original Sanskrit sutras with multiple translations, exploring their practical relevance to contemporary life and practice.

Requires some prior familiarity with yoga philosophy. Suitable for anyone with an inquiring mind who has completed an introductory course or has prior reading in the tradition.
