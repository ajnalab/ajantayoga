Who this is for

Yoga is for every body

You do not need flexibility, fitness, or a prior practice. You need only curiosity and a willingness to listen inward.

Ajanta Yoga Academy was founded on the belief that yoga's deepest gifts — clarity, equanimity, and a felt sense of wholeness — are available to everyone, including those who cannot practice a conventional asana sequence.

Our courses are specifically designed for practitioners who are managing chronic pain, recovering from injury, living with illness, or simply seeking a gentler, more philosophical entry into the tradition.
